X X V.
24 Februarii 1358.

Know all present and future that we, Thomas at See and
Simon at Hall, have given, granted, and, by this our present
charter, have confirmed to Simon Gilling, and Agnes, his
wife, three and a half acres of our land with their appur-
tenances lying in the town of East Sutton and Headcom,

In testimony whereof we have set our seals to this
present charter on Saturday the feast of S. Matthias the
Apostle in the thirty-second year of the reign of King
Edward, the third after the Conquest.

Witnesses, Richard de Lomherst; William at See;
